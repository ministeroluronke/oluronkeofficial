Ijaola

For Oluronke Ijaola it all began in her hometown, Ilesa, Osun State. There she functioned as Worship Leader of First Baptist Church, Ilesa. Oluronke faithfully lent her talents at her local assembly before she moved to Lagos, where she joined the Redeem Church. Most of her tenure at Redeem Church entailed leading worship weekly and managing the various entities of the Worship & Praise Department. More than a singing Artiste Oluronke is also passionate about teaching music, this led to the birth of her music academy
